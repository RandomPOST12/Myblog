What is CTF in terms of cybersecurity ?

Capture the Flag (CTF) in computer security is an exercise in which "flags" are secretly hidden in purposefully-vulnerable programs or websites. Competitors steal flags either from other competitors (attack/defense-style CTFs) or from the organizers (jeopardy-style challenges). Don't worry here you don't need to hack any application these CTF are very easy and made to give you a glimpse of actual CTF. These Labs are made focusing the knowledge of our participants.
